n8n Workflow: YouTube Video Transcription

Follow these steps to get a transcription of any YouTube video

To use this workflow, please do the following:

  1. Open your Gmail account.
  2. Compose a new email and send it to: joelmichaelgeorge@gmail.com
  3. In the email body, include the YouTube video URL you want transcribed, enclosed in angle brackets <>.
  4. Example: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc123>
  5. Once received, the workflow will automatically transcribe the video and email you the transcription.

Note: Make sure the URL is enclosed in angle brackets exactly as shown to ensure correct processing.
